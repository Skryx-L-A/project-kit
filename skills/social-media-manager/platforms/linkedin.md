# Platform — LinkedIn
- **Access**: LinkedIn Marketing/UGC API (approval-gated, OAuth) for company/personal posts, or
  browser automation fallback. Often drafts-only in practice.
- **Format**: hook line + line breaks + value, native (no outbound links in-body where reach
  suffers — put link in first comment). Documents/carousels perform well.
- **Tone**: professional, story/insight-led; B2B. Personal account reach > company page.
- **Engagement**: thoughtful comments on others' posts; reply to your commenters fast.
- **ToS**: no automation that mimics human bulk actions (connection/DM blasts get accounts
  restricted). Keep it manual-gated. Disclose sponsorship.
