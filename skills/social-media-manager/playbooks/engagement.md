# Playbook — Engagement & community

- **Speed**: reply within the first hour where possible (algorithms reward it).
- **Voice**: on-brand, human, helpful; never canned-sounding. No defensiveness.
- **Comments/DMs**: answer questions, thank praise, move buying-intent to a link/booking.
- **Community (Reddit/FB groups)**: contribute value first; promote rarely + within rules.
- **Escalation**: route complaints, legal threats, PR risks, and anything sensitive to a human
  (brand-safety-reviewer flags these). Don't argue publicly.
- **Moderation**: hide/report spam + abuse on owned posts per policy.
- **No** fake engagement, pod/vote rings, or mass identical replies.
