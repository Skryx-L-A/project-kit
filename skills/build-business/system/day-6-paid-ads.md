# Day 6 — Get clients with paid ads
**Goal:** make qualified owners come to you. Mass cold outreach fails (everyone can do it);
run paid ads modelled on proven winners.

**Do:**
1. Mine the free **Facebook Ad Library** for winning competitor ads in your market.
2. Run `prompts/6-ad-analyzer.md` with screenshots → winning patterns + 3 ad variants.
3. Save to `acquisition/ads.md`. Generate ad creatives with an image tool you have
   (e.g. the higgsfield skills) since Claude doesn't make images.
4. Note budget, targeting, and the offer in the ad.

**Output asset:** `acquisition/ads.md`.
