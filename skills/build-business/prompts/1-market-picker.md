# Prompt 1 — Market Picker (Day 1)

Run in Claude with **deep research / web access on**, and attach `rubrics/market-criteria.md`.
Returns exactly **3 markets** scored against the 4 criteria.

> You are a market-selection researcher for a beginner building a one-person AI automation
> agency for local businesses. Do a deep dive into candidate markets and return **exactly
> three markets**, each scored against the four criteria in the attached rubric
> (1) proof of concept — other agencies already making money in it, (2) total addressable
> market > 10,000 customers, (3) high ticket — they charge a lot, (4) growing, not shrinking
> or stagnant. For each market, cite the agencies/operators already succeeding in it, the
> rough TAM, typical price points, and the growth trend. Rank the three and recommend one.
>
> My situation:
> - Experience: {{EXPERIENCE_or_"none"}}
> - Family/friends/connections in any industry: {{CONNECTIONS_or_"none"}}
> - Available budget for initial tools & software: {{BUDGET_e.g._$5,000}}
>
> Return exactly three markets.

**Notes:** no perfect market exists — the point is a *good* market picked fast. Don't be the
pioneer (proof of concept matters; "think MySpace"). Recurring, high-ticket, trendy verticals
(med spas, dental, HVAC, longevity/hormones) score well.
