# Prompt 4 — Reviews & Referrals system (Day 4, Pillar 2)

> Write **every message** for our reviews-and-referrals system for a local business, and
> output **both SMS and email** versions of each message. The flow:
> 1. Text/email past & active customers, offer a raffle ({{RAFFLE e.g. $1,000 Amazon gift
>    card / 1-year free membership}}), ask them to rate experience **1–5**.
> 2. **4–5** → route to the Google review link (with the link).
> 3. **1–3** → route to a **private feedback form** (NOT Google); thank them, ask what to fix.
> 4. After a public review posts → AI thank-you response, then **immediately ask for a
>    referral**: a shareable link; each friend who signs up enters them in the raffle 5–10
>    more times.
>
> - Market: {{MARKET}}
> - Business name: {{BUSINESS_NAME}}

**Why:** ~97–98% read reviews before buying; 3.5→4.0 stars ≈ +5–10% revenue; only ~11% of
businesses ask. Best time to ask for a referral = right after public praise. More frequent,
recent, high reviews also lift local SEO ("<service> near me").

**Compliance:** consent + opt-out as in reactivation; never gate/buy reviews in violation of
Google policy — the raffle incentivises *leaving honest feedback*, not a positive review.
